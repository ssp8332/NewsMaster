Reliability and Bias of News Sources

We at NewsMaster strive to deliver you with reliable and unbiased news. To do this, we use a strict approval process to minimize bias and the risk of misinformation.

Stages of Approving a News Source:

  1. Checking the news source against Wikipedia's list of reliable sources to determine reliability. The news source is only added if it is reliable.
  2. Checking the news source against the Media Bias Fact Check database to determine political bias. If the news source has a moderate to high bias on either side of the political spectrum, then it will not be added to NewsMaster.
  3. Searching the internet for the general public's comments about a news source. A news source is only added if it is well-rated by the general public.
We hope you enjoy using NewsMaster! We always aim to provide you with the most reliable and trustworthy information.
